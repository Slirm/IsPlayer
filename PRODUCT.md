# Product

## Register

product

## Users

People who keep local videos on their Android device and want a calm, fast way to browse, import, and play them without a cloud account or heavy media-center setup.

## Product Purpose

IsPlayer is a lightweight local video library and player. Success means users can find a video quickly, understand where it lives, start playback confidently, and control playback without visual noise.

## Brand Personality

Restrained, cinematic, dependable.

## Anti-references

Avoid blue-purple AI gradients, overly playful empty states, over-rounded cards, heavy shadows, cluttered media-center chrome, and decorative motion that gets between the user and playback.

## Design Principles

- Keep the library quiet so thumbnails and titles carry the content.
- Use one confident accent for selection, progress, and primary actions.
- Prefer surface layers and clear spacing over shadows.
- Make empty, loading, and control states feel deliberate and recoverable.
- Preserve fast one-handed mobile operation with large touch targets.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Use readable contrast, 48dp minimum interactive targets where practical, clear content descriptions on actionable controls, text that survives font scaling, and short state transitions that do not distract from playback.
